# Seasonal Search Patterns for Bergen Event Queries

Research date: April 15, 2026

## Summary of Findings

Google Trends does not expose raw search volume via web search, so exact numbers are unavailable. However, combining tourism data, event calendars, cruise schedules, school holidays, and known Norwegian seasonal behavior, we can construct a reliable month-by-month content calendar with confidence about when search interest rises and peaks.

---

## Key Seasonal Queries — Timing Analysis

### 1. "hva skjer i bergen" (what's happening in Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Evergreen with summer peak. Highest in Jun-Aug when both locals and tourists search.
- **Prep window**: Peaks slowly from May, sharp rise in June.
- **Peak**: June-August
- **Action**: Evergreen collections (`denne-helgen`, `i-kveld`, `i-dag`) should always be optimized. English equivalents (`this-weekend`, `today-in-bergen`) matter most May-Sep.

### 2. "konserter bergen" (concerts Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Year-round with peaks around festival season (May-Jun) and autumn concert season (Sep-Nov).
- **Prep window**: April for spring/summer peak; August for autumn peak.
- **Peak**: May-June (festival stacking), September-November (indoor season).
- **Action**: `/konserter` collection is evergreen — ensure it has fresh SEO text.

### 3. "17 mai bergen" (May 17th — Constitution Day)
- **Pattern**: Extremely sharp spike. Near-zero interest most of the year, then rockets in late April / early May.
- **Prep window**: Mid-April (NOW)
- **Peak**: May 10-17 (search peaks ~1 week before the day)
- **URGENT**: Page must be fully optimized by April 25 at latest.
- **Collection**: `17-mai` / `17th-of-may-bergen` (date range: May 14-18)

### 4. "julemarked bergen" (Christmas market Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Sharp seasonal spike. Searches start mid-October, rise steeply in November, peak late Nov / early Dec.
- **Prep window**: September-October
- **Peak**: November 15 - December 10
- **Collection**: `julemarked` / `christmas-bergen` (Nov 15-Dec 23)
- **Note**: Gaari.no already ranks on page 1 for this (appeared in search results).

### 5. "sankthans bergen" (midsummer Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Very sharp spike around June 20-23. Zero interest otherwise.
- **Prep window**: Late May / early June
- **Peak**: June 18-23
- **URGENT**: Page should be optimized by May 20.
- **Collection**: `sankthans` / `midsummer-bergen` (Jun 21-24)

### 6. "paske bergen" (Easter Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Spike 2-3 weeks before Easter. Easter 2026 was April 5 (already passed).
- **Prep window**: March (for 2027)
- **Peak**: 2-3 weeks before Easter Sunday
- **Collection**: `paske` / `easter-bergen` — done for 2026, note for 2027 calendar.

### 7. "bergen events summer" / "things to do bergen" (English tourist queries)
- **Pattern**: Rises from April, peaks June-August. These are tourist-driven queries.
- **Prep window**: March-April (NOW for summer peak)
- **Peak**: June-August
- **URGENT**: English collection pages and meta descriptions should be summer-ready now.

### 8. "festival bergen"
- **Pattern**: Peaks mid-May through mid-June when Bergen's major festivals stack up.
- **Prep window**: April (NOW)
- **Peak**: May 20 - June 15 (Festspillene + Nattjazz + Bergenfest overlap)
- **URGENT**: All festival collection pages need to be current.

### 9. "hostferie bergen" / "vinterferie bergen" (school holidays)
- **Pattern**: Sharp spikes 2-3 weeks before each holiday.
- **Vinterferie 2027**: Week 9 (late Feb). Prep window: early February.
- **Hostferie 2026**: Week 41 (early Oct). Prep window: mid-September.
- **Collections**: `vinterferie` / `winter-break-bergen`, `hostferie`

### 10. "nyttarsaften bergen" (New Year's Eve Bergen)
- **Pattern**: Spike from December 20, peaks December 28-31.
- **Prep window**: Early December
- **Peak**: December 28-31
- **Collection**: `nyttarsaften` / `new-years-eve-bergen` (Dec 29-Jan 1)

## Tourism & Cruise Data

### Tourist Season
- **Peak**: June-August (largest visitor numbers)
- **Shoulder**: May, September (25-40% lower hotel rates, still significant traffic)
- **Low**: November-March (except Christmas and school holidays)
- **Best month overall**: June (longest days, festivals, bloom)

### Cruise Ship Season 2026
- **Season start**: Late March (first ships March 28)
- **Ramp-up**: April (several ships/week)
- **Peak**: June (highest density — 10+ ships in sample week of June 9-17)
- **High**: July-August
- **Wind-down**: September-October
- **Impact**: 680,000+ cruise passengers in 2025; limit 4 ships / 8,000 pax per day
- **Search implication**: Cruise passengers search "things to do in bergen" and "bergen events" 1-3 days before port call. English pages critical May-September.
